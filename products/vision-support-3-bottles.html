Eyetamins® by OPTASE® Vision Support™ is an eye health supplement formulated to support macular health and help maintain clear, sharp vision. Vision Support provides a comprehensive macular nutrition vision supplement in one well-tolerated, once-daily softgel, rather than single-nutrient eye vitamin. Built for daily use, it supports macular nutrition in one step.

  • DAILY VISION SUPPORT: Once-daily softgels with lutein, zinc contributing to normal visual function, zeaxanthin, and astaxanthin to help support macular health, enhance eye health and help maintain clear, sharp vision in one well-tolerated dose
  • CLINICALLY BACKED INGREDIENTS: Formulated with the trusted nutrients FloraGLO® lutein and AstaPure® astaxanthin, plus an independently tested formula for confidence in quality and consistency
  • ANTIOXIDANT: Carotenoids, like astaxanthin, along with Vitamin C, E, Zinc and Copper contribute to the protection of cells from oxidative stress. Vitamin D supports a normal inflammatory response, important in macular health

Use as directed.

  1. For adults one (1) softgel per day with a meal
  2. Do not exceed recommended daily dose.A dietary supplement is not a substitute for a healthy varied diet and lifestyle.

Softgels are designed for easy swallowing and a smooth, non-chalky experience while supporting bioavailability.

Each box includes 30 softgels for a 30-day supply. Softgel shell is made with bovine gelatin.

Allergen information: the product contains soybeans

Eyetamins® by OPTASE® is part of the OPTASE® Eye Care System.
