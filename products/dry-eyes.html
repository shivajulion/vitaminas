Eyetamins® by OPTASE® Eye Comfort™ is an omega-based eye supplement formulated to support moisture balance and eye comfort from within. The plant-based softgels fit easily into a daily routine, with a vegan, non GMO formula designed to align with a simple and consistent eye health routine.

Eyetamins® by OPTASE® is part of the OPTASE® Eye Care System. OPTASE® develops science-led, preservative-free eye care products with eye
specialists, with an eyecare routine that includes Heat, Cleanse, Hydrate, and Supplement to support comfort, hygiene, hydration, and nutrition.
 
At the core of the formula is organic sea buckthorn oil blend. The oil is included to support moisture balance and day-to-day comfort for eyes prone to dryness. Each serving (2 softgels) provides 800 mg fruit oil extract plus 200 mg seed oil. Sea buckthorn is a naturally rich source of vegan Omega-7. The Omega-7 formula works to support healthy tear production and moisture balance from within as part of long-term daily support. If you want omega nutrition but prefer to avoid fish oil, Eye Comfort™ offers a modern plant-based alternative with no fishy aftertaste.

The plant-based softgels fit easily into a daily routine, with a vegan, non-GMO formula designed to align with a simple and consistent eye health routine.

Take two (2) easy-to-swallow softgels once or twice daily with meals as directed for a simple habit that supports consistency and delivers well-tolerated, moisture support everyday.

Ingredients

Organic Sea Buckthorn Oil Blend, Modified Food Starch, Glycerin, Sorbitol, Carrageenan, Water
